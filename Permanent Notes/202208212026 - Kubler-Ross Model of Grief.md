# Kubler-Ross Model of Grief
**Topics:** [[202208212147 - Grief]]

Stage 1: Denial
Stage 2: Anger
Stage 3: Bargaining
Stage 4: Depression
Stage 5: Acceptance
- Alternatively, "Adaptation"
	- Much less passive tone and feels much less depressing!
	- Suggests active forging of a path ahead, as opposed to just giving up
